My Top 3 Favorite Places

  1. Arcadia Valley, Missouri
  2. Dillon, Colorado
  3. Gila Wilderness, New Mexico

Arcadia Valley, MO

Arcadia Valley is in southern Missouri and has become one of my favorite places to visit. I take my son down there as often as we can to go hiking on the beautiful Ozark Trail, and swimming along the St. Francis River. Our favorite places to visit are Taum Sauk State Park and Silver Mines Recreation Area.

Dillon, CO

Dillon is a small but popular mountain town that boasts a large lake and incredible mountain views. It is a must stop when traveling through Colorado on 70 at the very least.

Gila Wilderness, NM

The Gila Wilderness was the first designated wilderness area in the world. I backpacked with my cousins to the Jordan Hot Springs, hiking through canyons and rivers, and fell in love with how amazing the Gila is.
